report How to Report Harassment

  1. 1. Document details immediately (time, location, description)
  2. 2. Preserve any evidence (messages, photos, videos)
  3. 3. Report through this system or contact local authorities
  4. 4. Seek support from friends, family, or professional counselors
  5. 5. Follow up on your complaint until resolution
